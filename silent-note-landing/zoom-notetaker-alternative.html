Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using Velnot instead of Zoom AI Companion.

Does Velnot integrate with Zoom?

No integration needed. Velnot records system audio at the OS level, so it captures whatever is playing on your computer — including Zoom audio. Just start Velnot, then start your Zoom call.

Can other Zoom participants tell I'm recording?

No. Velnot doesn't interact with Zoom at all — it captures system audio silently. No notification is sent. Always comply with your local laws and company policies regarding call recording consent.

What if I lose my internet during the meeting?

No problem. Recording and storage are fully local. The transcription step requires internet, but the recording itself never does. Your audio is safe even if your connection drops.

Is the AI summarization private?

The transcript text (not audio) is sent to OpenAI's API for summarization. Your audio never leaves your machine. If you need fully offline operation, you can skip summarization and use the raw transcript.

Does this work with Zoom free accounts?

Yes. Velnot works independently of your Zoom plan. Whether you're on a free, Pro, or Enterprise Zoom account, Velnot records the same way — it doesn't connect to Zoom at all.
