Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Velnot with Microsoft Teams.

Do I need a Microsoft 365 subscription to use Velnot?

No. Velnot works completely independently of your Microsoft subscription. It records system audio at the OS level — it doesn't connect to Teams, SharePoint, or any Microsoft service.

Will Teams participants see a recording notification?

No. Velnot doesn't trigger Teams' recording system. It captures audio at the OS level, so Teams has no idea you're recording. Always check your local laws and employer policies regarding meeting recording.

Can my IT department see that I'm using Velnot?

Velnot is a local Windows app that doesn't communicate with any corporate infrastructure. It's no different from opening a text editor while on a Teams call. No network traffic, no corporate visibility.

What happens to the audio after transcription?

The audio stays on your computer. If you have "auto-delete audio" enabled in settings, the recording is deleted after transcription — leaving only the transcript and summary. Your data, your choice.

Does this work with Teams on the web or just the desktop app?

Velnot works with both. As long as audio is playing through your Windows system, Velnot captures it — whether Teams is in the desktop app, Edge, or Chrome.
